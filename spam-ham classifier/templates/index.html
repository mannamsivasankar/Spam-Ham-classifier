Spam-Ham Classifier

Spam Sentinel

Classifies messages as spam or legitimate ham. Engineered with TfidfVectorizer and Multinomial Naive Bayes.

  • Accuracy: 96.77%
  • Feature Set: TF-IDF Word Vectorization
  • Target: Spam vs Ham SMS Messages
Tweet Safety Classifier

Tweet Guard

Moderates Twitter posts into Hate Speech, Offensive Language, or Normal categories. Implemented with NLTK lemmatization, CountVectorizer, SelectPercentile (35% feature reduction), and a Linear SVC classifier.

  • Accuracy: 92.02%
  • Feature Selection: Chi-2 Select Percentile (Top 65% retained)
  • Categories: Hate Speech, Offensive, Normal
